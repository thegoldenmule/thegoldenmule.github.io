I’ve been fooling around with the Away3d 4.0-beta lately (for FP11!). I wrote a quick demo–it’s using Box2D on the XZ plane, and I’m faking the Y physics. Try it out!

**Arrow Keys** move.

**Spacebar** shoots.

**Enter** jumps.

If you time it right, you can jump over the wall!
